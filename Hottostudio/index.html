about

Hottostudio are Pierpaolo Di Giuseppe, Gianluigi Cerioli and Carlotta MendolaThree different personality with three different backgrounds linked in one language, approach and vision, deal with graphic design, product and photography.Our guidelines are minimalist, contemporary and sensorial. We propose visual identity, communication strategy and art direction. Follow the client to achieve the goal step by step. Our commissions range from large brands to smaller startups. Hottostudio is based in Novara but work between Piedmont and Lombardy.
Design born to last.
